# IPTV Supreme Player - Support

Welcome to IPTV Supreme Player, the fast and smooth IPTV streaming solution for iOS and Android.

## Features

- **Profile Management**: Create and manage multiple IPTV profiles (Xtream Code & M3U URL)
- **Live TV, VOD & Series**: Access all your content in one place
- **Universal Search**: Search across channels, movies, and series instantly
- **EPG Guide**: View program schedules and descriptions
- **Audio & Subtitle Tracks**: Switch between available tracks on-the-fly
- **Favorites**: Save your preferred content for quick access
- **Multi-language**: Available in English, French, Spanish, Portuguese, Italian, and German
- **Stream Formats**: Choose between TS and M3U8 protocols

## Getting Started

### Creating Your First Profile

1. Open the app and tap **"Create New Profile"**
2. Choose your profile type:
   - **Xtream Code**: Enter server URL, username, and password
   - **M3U URL**: Enter your M3U playlist URL
3. Select your preferred stream format (TS or M3U8)
4. Tap **"Create"** and wait for content to load

### Navigation

- **Channels**: Browse live TV channels by category
- **Series**: Explore TV shows with seasons and episodes
- **VOD**: Watch on-demand movies
- **Favorites**: Quick access to saved content
- **Settings**: Manage profiles and app preferences

## Troubleshooting

### Content Not Loading

- Verify your profile credentials are correct
- Check your internet connection
- Try switching stream format (Settings â†’ Stream Format)
- Refresh the content by pulling down

### Playback Issues

- **Buffering**: Switch from M3U8 to TS (or vice versa)
- **No Video**: Check if your provider's stream is active
- **Audio/Subtitle Issues**: Use the track selector buttons in the player

### Connection Timeout

The app automatically retries failed connections. If issues persist:
- Contact your IPTV provider to verify service status
- Check if your account has active connections remaining

- Restore purchases on new devices using **"Restore Purchases"**.
