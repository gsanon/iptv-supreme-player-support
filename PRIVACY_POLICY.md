# Privacy Policy for IPTV Supreme Player

**Last Updated**: January 7, 2026

## Overview

IPTV Supreme Player ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

## Data Collection and Storage

### What We Store Locally

The App stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- **IPTV Profiles**: Profile names, server URLs, usernames, and passwords
- **User Preferences**: Language settings, stream format preferences, adult content filter settings
- **Favorites**: Your saved channels, movies, and series
- **Premium Status**: In-app purchase information

### What We Do NOT Collect

**We do not collect, access, transmit, or store any of your data on our servers.** All information remains exclusively on your device.

- ❌ No personal information collected
- ❌ No IPTV credentials transmitted to us
- ❌ No usage analytics or tracking
- ❌ No browsing history
- ❌ No viewing habits
- ❌ No location data

## Third-Party Services

### In-App Purchases

The App uses Apple App Store and Google Play Store for in-app purchases (ad removal). These transactions are processed entirely by Apple/Google, and we only receive confirmation of purchase status. Please refer to:

- [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)
- [Google Play Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### IPTV Provider Connections

When you use the App to stream content, you connect directly to your IPTV provider's servers. We are not involved in these connections and do not have access to:

- Your streaming activity
- Content you watch
- Your provider's data

**Note**: Your IPTV provider may have their own privacy policy regarding data collection. Please contact them for details.

## Data Security

All data is stored securely on your device using platform-standard secure storage mechanisms:

- iOS: Keychain and UserDefaults
- Android: Encrypted SharedPreferences

## Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

- **Delete Data**: Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data
- **Delete Profiles**: Remove individual profiles anytime via Settings
- **Modify Settings**: Change preferences at any time

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. Parental discretion is advised for content filtering (adult content settings).
